This is the Sauerbraten 3D FPS compiled from C++ and OpenGL to JavaScript and WebGL using Emscripten. Code is zlib licensed and on github.

The demo might take time to download. Afterwards, press the "fullscreen" button and then "GO!". Move with WASD, jump with space, look around with the mouse. You can shoot a little by clicking the mouse. Some editing stuff works (press 'e' and fly around, drag the acorn, etc). You can change the resolution in the menu options (Options->Res tab). Add a bot by bringing up the main menu (` or escape) and selecting "add bot". Then walk around the bot to initialize the AI.

This is a very very early demo - ignore performance and content quality! Notes:

  • Very little optimization for speed has been done
  • The level you see when you press "GO!" was made by azakai, a person with 0 artistic talent
  • The game assets (textures) have not been optimized for faster downloads at all
  • Very recent development versions of browsers are needed to get all functionality to work:
    • Fullscreen is known to work in at least Firefox 15+ and Chrome 20+
    • Mouse lock is known to work in at least Firefox 15+, looks like for Chrome it is planned for Chrome 21
    • Sound is known to work in Firefox, but fails in Chrome due to a problem with BlobBuilder
